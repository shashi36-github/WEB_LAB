Sports at VIT-AP

About Sports at VIT-AP

At SK, we believe that sound mental and good physical health are crucial for holistic development. With a strong emphasis on recreation, we provide facilities that keep our students, faculty, and staff active, spirited, involved, and healthy. We organize several intramural sports events, including cricket, badminton, volleyball, chess, and more. Additionally, we host the in-house Sports and Cultural fest VITopia where different colleges and universities participate.

International Karate Championship

Our university students (boys & girls) participated in the 4th International Karate Championship held from 31/01/2020 to 02/02/2020 at Rajivgandhi Port Stadium Visakhapatnam, organized by the UNITED SHOTOKAN KARATE-DO INDIA. Over 700 contestants participated in various categories. We are proud to announce that our team won a total of 13 medals.

Hostels

The SK hostels are highly secure and well guarded. Only SKians & SK authorized personnel are allowed inside. We aspire to make the hostel a ‘home away from home. Each hostel room is equipped with a cot, chair, study table and cupboard. Resident wardens are available 24×7. Provision stores are located in the hostel where students can buy essentials such as mattresses, pillows, buckets, mugs, locks, stationeries, books, soaps. Both AC and Non-AC rooms are available. The type of room is allocated on the first-come first serve basis and availability once the hostel fee is paid in full.
