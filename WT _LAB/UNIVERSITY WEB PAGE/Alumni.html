Alumni Affairs

Overview

The Directorate of Alumni Affairs was established in September 2017 to look after the alumni needs for the entire University. It was earlier functioning as the SKEC Alumni Office since 1996. The major objectives of the Directorate are to enhance the coordination between SK University and its alumni, to improve the interaction of alumni with the students and the faculty members, to establish alumni chapters in India and abroad, to maintain the database of alumni and to look after the interests of SK alumni.


Vision

Develop a fully connected, strong, SK alumni community.

Mission

Advance SK Institute of Science and Technology by promoting alumni relationship fostering commitment among students enabling student-alumni interactions.
